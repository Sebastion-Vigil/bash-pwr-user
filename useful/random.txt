## Toggling Console Mode/GUI Mode in Linux:
- To switch to Console mode Press ‘Ctrl + Alt + F1' (F2 -- F6) 
- To switch between consoles in Console mode Press ‘Alt + F1' (F2 -- F6) 
- To switch to GUI mode Press ‘Alt + F7'
